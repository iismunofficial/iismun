THE VISION

At IIS MUN, we believe Model United Nations is more than speeches and blocs. It is about critical thinking, informed dialogue, and responsible leadership. Our vision is to provide a platform where students engage deeply with global issues, represent perspectives with integrity, and learn the value of diplomacy over dominance. We strive to make IIS MUN inclusive, intellectually challenging, and meaningful for every delegate. Whether it is a first-time participant or an experienced MUNer, we aim to foster confidence, clarity of thought, and respect for diverse viewpoints. IIS MUN is a space where ideas lead, voices matter, and future leaders are shaped through dialogue and action.

Ms.Sakina Lakdawala

Principal, Innovative International School

What is MUN?

Model United Nations is an academic simulation where students represent different spots across the globe as diplomats. Delegates research, debate, and negotiate to find solutions to global complexities, fostering leadership and public speaking skills.

  • ● Global Awareness
  • ● Diplomatic Research
  • ● Public Speaking Excellence

COMMITTEES

The Forums of Governance

UNSC

International

United Nations Security Council: Responsible for maintaining international peace and security under Chapter V of the UN Charter, with powers including sanctions and peacekeeping.

LOK SABHA

National

House of People: Enacts national laws, controls public finances, and holds the Union Government accountable through rigorous parliamentary oversight.

GLA

State

Gujarat Legislative Assembly: Legislates on state subjects, approves state budgets, and represents regional interests within the Indian constitutional framework.

INT. PRESS

Journalism

Documents proceedings, analyzes debates, and ensures transparency through accurate and fair responsible journalistic coverage of the conference.
