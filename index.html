What is
I.I.S MUN?

Students act as diplomats, embodying various nations and spots from around the world to discuss global issues authentically. Through research and diplomatic processes, delegates collaboratively seek solutions to the world's most pressing challenges.

COMMITTEES

Diverse bodies representing global and national governance.

UNSC

United Nations Security Council

Under Chapter V of the UN Charter, the Security Council is responsible for maintaining international peace and security.

Lok Sabha

House of the People

Enacts national laws, controls public finances, and holds the Union Government accountable through debate.

GLA

Gujarat Legislative Assembly

Legislates on state subjects, approves the state budget, and represents regional interests.

IP

International Press

Documents proceedings and ensures transparency through fair responsible journalistic coverage.
